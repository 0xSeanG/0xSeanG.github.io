Source: [https://www.mostlyhomemademom.com/smoked-sweet-kielbasa-bites/](https://www.mostlyhomemademom.com/smoked-sweet-kielbasa-bites/) Servings: 8 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 1 hr 30 min

### Ingredients

* 14 oz smoked kielbasa, sliced into ½-inch rounds
* ¾ cup ginger ale
* ½ cup packed brown sugar
* ½ cup barbecue sauce

### Directions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
2. Whisk together ginger ale, brown sugar, and barbecue sauce until combined.
3. Place kielbasa slices in a single layer in a baking dish.
4. Pour sauce mixture over kielbasa.
5. Bake uncovered for 1½ hours, stirring once or twice, until sauce has thickened and caramelized.
6. Serve warm.
