### Ingredients

* 2 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
* 1 (8 oz) package shredded cheddar cheese
* 1 tbsp chopped onion
* 1 tbsp chopped green bell pepper
* 1 tbsp chopped red bell pepper
* Squirt of Worcestershire sauce
* Squirt of real lemon juice
* Finely chopped walnuts, enough to coat

### Directions

1. Mix cream cheese, cheddar, onion, green pepper, red pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice together really well.
2. Form into a ball.
3. Roll ball in finely chopped walnuts until fully coated.
4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
