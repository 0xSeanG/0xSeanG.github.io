### Ingredients

* 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
* 1 tbsp olive oil
* ½ tsp garlic powder
* ½ tsp salt
* ½ tsp black pepper

#### Cheese Sauce

* 2 tbsp unsalted butter
* 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
* 2 cups whole milk
* ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
* Salt and pepper, to taste
* 1½ cups shredded Gruyère cheese, divided

#### Topping

* ¼ cup panko breadcrumbs

### Directions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss cauliflower florets with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet.
3. Roast for 20 minutes, until lightly golden. Reduce oven to 375°F.
4. Meanwhile, make the cheese sauce: Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook 1–2 minutes. Gradually whisk in milk. Cook, stirring constantly, until thickened, about 5 minutes. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat and stir in 1 cup of the Gruyère.
5. Arrange roasted cauliflower in an 8x11 baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over the top.
6. Sprinkle remaining ½ cup Gruyère and panko breadcrumbs over the top.
7. Bake at 375°F for 25–30 minutes, until bubbly and golden brown.
