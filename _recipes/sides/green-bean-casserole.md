### Ingredients

#### Crispy Onion Topping

* Avocado oil, for frying
* 1 cup cassava flour
* 1 tbsp arrowroot flour
* Salt, to taste
* 1 large onion, thinly sliced into rings

#### Casserole

* 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
* 2 jars Primal Kitchen Cashew Kale Pesto Alfredo Sauce
* Crispy onion topping (from above)

### Directions

#### Crispy Onion Topping

1. Heat 1–2 inches of avocado oil in a heavy-bottomed pot or skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Whisk together cassava flour, arrowroot flour, and salt in a shallow bowl.
3. Toss onion rings in the flour mixture to coat.
4. Fry in batches until golden and crispy, about 3–4 minutes. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

#### Casserole

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Blanch green beans in boiling salted water for 3–4 minutes; drain.
3. Combine green beans and both jars of Cashew Kale Pesto Alfredo Sauce in a 9x13 baking dish. Mix well.
4. Bake uncovered for 25–30 minutes, until bubbling.
5. Top with crispy onion rings and bake an additional 5–10 minutes until topping is set.
