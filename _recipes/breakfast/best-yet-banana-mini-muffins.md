Source: Allrecipes.com (submitted by cathy) Yield: 48 mini muffins | Prep: 20 min | Cook: 15 min | Total: 35 min

### Ingredients

* Cooking spray
* 1½ cups all-purpose flour
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 3 bananas, mashed
* ¾ cup white sugar
* ⅓ cup melted butter
* 1 egg, beaten

### Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease 2 mini muffin tins with cooking spray.
2. Combine flour, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, blend mashed bananas, sugar, butter, and egg together. Add to flour mixture and stir well to combine.
4. Scoop batter into prepared muffin cups, filling each ⅔ full.
5. Bake about 15 minutes, until tops spring back when lightly pressed.
6. Cool in tins for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
